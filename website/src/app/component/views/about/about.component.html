I tell my students, 'When you get these jobs that you have been so brilliantly trained for, just remember that your real job is that if you are free, you need to free somebody else. If you have some power, then your job is to empower somebody else. This is not just a grab-bag candy game.” - Toni Morrison

As Toni Morrison said in the quote above, everybody has the power and opportunity to empower others. I believe that it is our duty to support and help other people who may not necessarily have the means to support themselves.

Bringing this idea into computer science and software development, I aim to use my software development skills to enrich and better the lives of people. I want to utilize my skillset such that it can help streamline user experience and produce impactful software to create a more sustainable environment.

My career interests lie in front-end software development, big data, and data science. As a previous neuroscience major, I am also interested in bioinformatics, genomics, and molecular biology.

About
