User-Centered Design & Analysis, Fall 2021

Project Name
BusyCooker
Project Description

BusyCooker is a mobile application that helps busy people who spend a lot of money eating out with low-effort, quick, and healthy recipes, and track how much money they save by not eating out. Nowadays, people sacrifice their cooking time to continue working for their job, school, etc., by either skipping meals or eating out for convenience. This application suggests recipes that are quick and easy to make to help people find a better balance between their meals and work while also keeping them accountable by tracking what meals they cook at home and how much money they save by doing so.
